3D Topography (10th to 60th Percentiles)

A 3D column plot of 200 representative paths slicing from the 10th to 60th percentiles. The column height represents portfolio balance. The color reflects the annual market return for that year (Red = crash, Green = boom). Rotate and zoom freely; single-click a column to pop that whole simulation up above the others and pin it, then mouse over each year for its values. Click the same column again, or any empty space, to release.
