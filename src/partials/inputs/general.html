Sequence of Returns Planning

Maximize realistic withdrawals within your risk tolerance. The focus is investment drawdown tradeoffs - accounts, budgets, RMDs, pensions, Social Security, real estate, etc. are out of scope. Everything in this tool — portfolio balances, withdrawals, and market returns — uses real (today’s) dollars, so you don’t need to consider inflation. Market returns and inflation are based on historical data.

Use a range of years around the endpoint. Leave both at 0 for a fixed timeline.
