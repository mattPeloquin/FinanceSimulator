Balance-Based Spending Scale

Smoothly scale your total withdrawal based on portfolio balance: below the floor, spending ramps down toward the max cut (reached at $0); above the ceiling, it ramps up by the boost rate for every extra multiple of the ceiling — with no upper cap. Between the two, spending is unchanged. Set a minimum withdrawal to always stress-test depletion, even when the scale would cut spending.
