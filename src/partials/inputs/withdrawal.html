Spending Over Time

Stage how your base withdrawal changes over retirement. Each tier's annual real change compounds continuously (negative = spend more now, less later); extra withdrawal adds a flat amount on top for that tier's years. Set any value to 0. Add tiers to stage different patterns — the last tier applies to all remaining years. Remove all but one tier for a flat schedule. When Goal Seek includes this section, it tunes only the first tier's extra withdrawal.

Gifting

Add an extra gift withdrawal in years when your portfolio balance (after growth and your regular withdrawal or deposit) exceeds the tier's balance threshold and that year's spending fully met your plan — guardrails or penalties did not cut it short. Add tiers to stage different gifts over time — the last tier applies to all remaining years. Set Gift to 0 (or leave Balance blank) on early tiers to defer gifting to later years. Remove all tiers for no gifting. The chart below shows a dotted gift ceiling above your planned withdrawal.
