Fees & Taxes

Enable to report plan withdrawals net of payments, otherwise plan withdrawals assume tax and fee payments baked in.

%

Percent of portfolio each year (advisor fee and/or fund expenses). Applied after market growth, even in down years.

Withdrawal tax over time

Stage an effective tax on spending you withdraw. Amounts in Withdrawal Strategy are what you spend or receive; the portfolio withdraws more to cover tax (tax ≈ rate × net). Glide surplus is always taxed. Gifts are taxed only when the tier checkbox is on (e.g. gifts to children). Major events are not taxed here. Optional nested spending levels apply higher rates only to net spending above each threshold.
