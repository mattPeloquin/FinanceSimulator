Explore specific paths

A 3D plot of representative paths from the 5th to 65th percentiles. Column height represents portfolio balance, color reflects the annual market return for that year (Red = crash, Green = boom). Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, right-drag to pan. Single-click a column to pin that simulation and mouse over each year for values; click the column again or empty space to release. Double-click a column to explore ~200 nearby simulations; double-click again to return.

Sequence Risk: IRR vs Average Return

Each dot is one simulation. Dots below the dashed diagonal were hurt by early bad years — their money-weighted IRR trails their time-weighted average return; dots above it got lucky early. Paths in the lower-right had good average returns but still fell short of the plan: sequence risk in its purest form. Click a dot to see that path's withdrawals against its plan.
