Explore specific paths

A 3D plot of representative paths from the 10th to 60th percentiles. Column height represents portfolio balance, color reflects the annual market return for that year (Red = crash, Green = boom). Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, right-drag to pan. Single-click a column to pin that simulation and mouse over each year for values; click the column again or empty space to release. Double-click a column to explore ~200 nearby simulations; double-click again to return.
