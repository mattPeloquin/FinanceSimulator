3D Topography (10th to 60th Percentiles)

A 3D column plot of 200 representative paths slicing from the 10th to 60th percentiles. The column height represents portfolio balance. The color reflects the annual market return for that year (Red = crash, Green = boom). Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, right-drag to pan. Single-click a column to pin that simulation and mouse over each year for values; click the column again or empty space to release.
